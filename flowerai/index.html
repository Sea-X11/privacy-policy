FlowerAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

FlowerAI is designed to provide AI-assisted flower and plant guidance while keeping the experience simple and privacy-conscious.

Information We Process

When you use the Ask AI feature, the content you submit may be processed by third-party AI service providers in order to generate responses.

This may include:

  • Text questions you enter
  • Photos you optionally attach for image-assisted analysis

These requests are used only to provide the app’s core functionality.

Local History

FlowerAI stores your recent question and answer history locally on your device so you can revisit previous conversations.

We do not require account registration for the core app experience.

Public Content Sources

FlowerAI may load flower summaries and images from publicly available sources such as Wikipedia and Wikimedia.

Product Recommendations

Some recommendations may open external shopping links, including Amazon affiliate search links.

FlowerAI does not sell your personal information or use uploaded photos for advertising purposes.

Tracking and Advertising

FlowerAI does not use third-party advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking technologies.

We do not use your uploaded photos or questions to build advertising profiles.

Data Security

We aim to minimize data collection wherever possible and only use third-party services necessary to provide AI-powered responses and app functionality.
