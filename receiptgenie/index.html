Privacy Policy

ReceiptGenie / 票据精灵 / レシート精霊  ·  Last updated: April 27, 2026

ReceiptGenie processes receipt images and OCR results locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or sell your personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Receipt images, OCR text, or exported documents
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers, analytics, or tracking data
  • Behavioral usage profiles

2. On-Device Processing Only

Receipt scanning, OCR recognition, categorization, and export preparation are processed locally on your device. We do not operate a backend service that stores your receipt content.

3. What ReceiptGenie Stores Locally

ReceiptGenie may store the following data on your device only:

  • Receipt images that you scan or import
  • OCR-extracted fields such as merchant, date, amount, and currency
  • Notes, categories, and local display preferences
  • Export quota status for the free plan
  • Exported files that you choose to generate

4. Camera and Photo Library Access

If you scan a receipt, the app requests camera access solely to capture the receipt image. If you import a receipt, the app requests Photo Library access solely to retrieve the image you select. We do not access, upload, or retain any media beyond the items you explicitly choose to use inside the app.

5. Sharing and Export

When you export to Excel, Word, or PDF and use the iOS share sheet, the selected file is passed only to the destination you choose. This action is initiated entirely by you.

6. In-App Purchases

ReceiptGenie offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade. Purchase transactions are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple’s privacy policy governs purchase and billing data: apple.com/legal/privacy.

7. Third-Party Services

ReceiptGenie does not integrate third-party advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

8. Children's Privacy

ReceiptGenie does not knowingly collect personal information from children because the app does not collect personal information from users at all.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via: github.com/Sea-X11
