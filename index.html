Privacy Policy

DailyLog  ·  Last updated: April 23, 2026

DailyLog processes diary content and emotional analysis locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or sell your personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Diary text or emotional records
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers or analytics
  • Behavioral tracking data

2. On-Device Processing Only

All diary content, emotion analysis, keyword extraction, and trend summaries are processed locally on your device. We do not operate a backend service that stores your diary data.

3. What DailyLog Stores Locally

DailyLog may store the following information on your device only:

  • Diary text you write
  • Emotion scores and labels generated on device
  • Keywords, trends, and insight summaries generated on device
  • Local settings and feature usage state

4. In-App Purchases

DailyLog offers optional Pro subscriptions. Subscription transactions are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple’s privacy policy governs purchase and billing data: apple.com/legal/privacy.

5. Local Usage Tracking

For free-tier users, certain feature limits are tracked locally on your device using Apple’s UserDefaults. This information never leaves your device.

6. Sharing

If you use the in-app share feature, the content you choose to share is passed to the iOS share sheet and then to the destination you select. That action is initiated entirely by you.

7. Third-Party Services

DailyLog does not integrate third-party advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

8. Children's Privacy

DailyLog does not knowingly collect any personal information from children because we do not collect personal information from users at all.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the App Store listing for DailyLog or via: github.com/Sea-X11
