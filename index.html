Privacy Policy

xuVideoConverter  ·  Last updated: March 26, 2026

xuVideoConverter processes all videos entirely on your device. No video files, personal data, or usage information are ever sent to external servers.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Video files or media content
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage patterns or behavioral data

2. On-Device Processing Only

All video conversion happens locally on your device using Apple's AVFoundation framework. Converted files are saved directly to your device's local storage or Files app. No content leaves your device at any time.

3. In-App Purchases

xuVideoConverter offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade. Purchase transactions are processed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase data: apple.com/legal/privacy.

4. Daily Usage Tracking

For free-tier users, a daily conversion counter is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This counter never leaves your device and is reset automatically every 24 hours.

5. Photo Library Access

If you choose to import videos from your Photos library, the app requests read access solely to retrieve the selected video files. We do not access, copy, or retain any other photos or metadata beyond what you explicitly select.

6. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
