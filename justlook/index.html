Privacy Policy

JustLook  ·  Last updated: May 2, 2026

JustLook is a free card game. We do not require account creation, we do not sell personal information, and basic gameplay works offline.

1. Information We Do Not Directly Collect

JustLook does not directly collect, store, or sell personal information such as:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Payment details
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Cross-app tracking data

2. Local Device Data

JustLook may store limited local app state on your device, such as gameplay state, interface preferences, and whether you have already viewed the rules screen. This information is stored locally on your device.

3. Game Center

JustLook supports Apple Game Center for online matchmaking and friend invites. If you use Game Center, Apple may process information such as your Game Center display name, player identifier, matchmaking state, and multiplayer session data according to Apple's own privacy practices.

Apple's privacy policy is available here: apple.com/legal/privacy

4. Advertising and Tracking

JustLook does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

5. In-App Purchases

JustLook does not currently offer paid subscriptions or in-app purchases.

6. Children's Privacy

JustLook does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the project here: github.com/Sea-X11/JustLook

简体中文说明

JustLook 是一款免费的卡牌游戏。应用本身不要求你注册账号，也不会直接收集你的姓名、邮箱、电话、支付信息或广告追踪数据。

应用可能会在你的设备本地保存少量游戏状态和界面偏好，例如是否已经查看规则页。这些内容仅保存在你的设备上。

如果你使用 Game Center 联机功能，Apple 可能会根据其服务规则处理你的 Game Center 昵称、玩家标识和联机对局相关数据。

JustLook 当前不接入第三方广告 SDK，不进行跨 App 追踪，也没有内购或订阅。
